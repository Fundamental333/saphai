SAPH-AI: Security, Agency, Privacy of Human-AI

SAPH-AI is an interdisciplinary workshop on privacy, security, agency, identity, and governance in human-AI ecosystems, with a focus on conversational AI, generative systems, and agentic technologies.

About

SAPH-AI brings together researchers and practitioners interested in how security, agency, and privacy jointly shape human-AI interaction.

Conversational AI, generative systems, and autonomous agents increasingly process sensitive data, build persistent user representations, and act on behalf of users across tools, platforms, and institutional contexts.

The workshop examines these systems through the connected lenses of privacy, security, identity, and governance. It aims to support interdisciplinary discussion across HCI, security, privacy, AI systems, social computing, and policy.

Topics of Interest

We welcome work on privacy, security, agency, identity, and governance in conversational and agentic AI systems.

  • Privacy and security risks in conversational and agentic AI
  • Identity inference, behavioral profiling, and persistent memory
  • Multimodal leakage and cross-platform data exposure
  • Delegated decision making, user autonomy, and agency
  • Transparency, auditability, accountability, and governance
  • Privacy-centered design and safeguards for AI assistants and autonomous systems
  • Human-centered evaluations of AI copilots, chatbots, and agentic systems
  • Impacts on vulnerable or historically marginalized populations

Call for Participation

SAPH-AI invites participation from researchers and practitioners across HCI, privacy, security, AI systems, design, and governance.

What We Welcome

  • Research papers and position papers
  • Early-stage ideas and design provocations
  • Privacy-centered prototypes for human-AI systems
  • Empirical, technical, design, or policy perspectives
The workshop is intended as an interdisciplinary forum for emerging ideas, constructive discussion, and community building around human-AI ecosystems.

Workshop Format

SAPH-AI is planned as a full-day interactive workshop.

  • Welcome and framing session
  • Invited keynote
  • Paper and position paper presentations
  • Prototype and design discussion
  • Collaborative breakout sessions
  • Closing synthesis and research agenda building
